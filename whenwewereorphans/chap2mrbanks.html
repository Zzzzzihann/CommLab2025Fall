Father

File No.02

• Born in London in 1865, initially worked in the banking sector before joining Morgan Brook & Byatt Company in 1890.

• After marrying his wife in 1897, he relocated to Shanghai due to a position transfer and became involved in the company’s trade operations between British India and China.

• During his years in Shanghai, he frequently appeared in social circles within the International Settlement and was known for his fondness for horse racing.

• On the last recorded occasion, he was seen wearing a white suit and white hat, carrying a briefcase and a cane.
