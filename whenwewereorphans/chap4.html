It has been 25 years since you returned to London.
But through school, through work, through every case you solved—
you always felt like a guest here, never rooted, never whole.
For across the sea, in Shanghai, your parents remain unfound.
And your mother—somewhere in that city—still waits for you.
You know that if you return—if you find the old servants, the neighbours, the friends—
you will uncover the thread that leads you back to them.
But the truth hides like fog, shifting as you approach.
Memories do not always return the shape they once held.
You know this, and yet you hesitate.
You have a life here now. A name. A reputation.
Will you still return to Shanghai—now broken by war?
Do you still wish to find them?
