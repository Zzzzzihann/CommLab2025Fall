Family

File No.01

• The Banks couple moved from London to Shanghai in 1897 due to Mr. Banks’s professional assignment.

• The family lived in a garden villa characterized by a long curved staircase and a second-floor balcony.

• Besides their son Christopher, who was born in Shanghai, the household also included several Chinese servants, among them a maid named Mei Li from Shandong, China.

• The Banks family had no immediate relatives remaining in England, with Mrs. Banks’s cousin, Cecilia Roberts, being the only known family member still residing in England.
