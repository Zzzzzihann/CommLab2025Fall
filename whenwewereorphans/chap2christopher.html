File No.00

• Christopher Banks, born in Shanghai, lived in Shanghai International Settlement with his parents. He had a childhood friend named Akira.

• He was sent to England at the age of nine following the disappearance of his parents and was placed under the guardianship of his aunt, Cecilia, in London.

• He later attended Cambridge University. Influenced by his parents’ unresolved case, he developed an early interest in criminal investigation and eventually became a well-known private detective after graduation.

• Unmarried, he is the adoptive father of Jennifer, an orphan whose parents died in a maritime accident; she was briefly cared for by relatives in Canada before the adoption was completed.

• Despite professional success, one unresolved matter remains in his personal record: the disappearance of his parents in Shanghai.

• In recent years he has revisited surviving letters, newspaper clippings, and early testimonies, and now anticipates returning to Shanghai in hopes of reconstructing the remaining evidence.
