The Shanghai Evening Post

— July 2nd, 1912 —

In Shanghai, 1911

Missing Persons Notice

Mr. and Mrs. Banks, British nationals, have recently disappeared in succession. Any information from the public is hereby requested.

Mr. Banks was last seen ten days ago, reportedly leaving home in the morning to travel to the offices of Morgan Brook & Byatt Company. He was wearing a white suit. The company has offered no confirmation nor denial regarding his whereabouts;

Mrs. Banks filed a report with the authorities following her husband's failure to return. Four days ago, while walking with their young son in the Shanghai International Settlement, she too went missing. She was wearing dark blue dress.

Christopher Banks, their nine years old son, remains at home awaiting their return.

Should any individual hold recollections — however uncertain — please contact authorities or Mr. Banks' colleague Philip S.

A fragment of memory may contain the truth we have lost.
